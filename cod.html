    Call of Duty (COD) is a fast-paced first-person shooter that tests your reflexes, strategy, and teamwork. Players compete in intense multiplayer matches across different modes like Team Deathmatch, Search & Destroy, and Domination. Each match requires quick decision-making, map awareness, and precise aiming skills.

    In our tournaments, players can participate solo or as a team, depending on the format. Matches are conducted in custom lobbies, and participants must join using the provided tournament code before the match begins. Make sure to check the schedule and be ready at least 10 minutes early.

    🔥 Tips: - Communicate with your team for better coordination - Choose loadouts based on map and mode - Stay aware of enemy positions using sound and minimap
